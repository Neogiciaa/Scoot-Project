FAQs

How it works

  • How do I download the app?

    To download the Scoot app, you can search "Scoot" in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you'll be re-directed to the correct page.

  • Can I find a nearby Scoots?

    Yes, the app allows you to locate nearby scooters easily. Open the app and it will show the nearest Scoot locations on the map.

  • Do I need a license to ride?

    No, you don't need a specific license to ride, but you must follow the local laws and regulations.

Safe driving

  • Should I wear a helmet?

    Yes, please do! All cities have different laws. But we strongly strongly strongly recommend always wearing a helmet regardless of the local laws. We like you and we want you to be as safe as possible while Scooting.

  • How about the rules and regulations?

    It's important to follow all local traffic laws while riding. Be sure to stay informed about specific regulations in your area.

  • What if I damage my scoot?

    If you damage your scooter, please report it via the app immediately. Our team will assist you with the next steps.
